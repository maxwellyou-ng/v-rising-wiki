Solarus the Immaculate

From V Rising Wiki

Solarus the Immaculate is a level 86 V Blood boss found in the Fortress of Light in the northern reaches of Silverlight Hills. He is the final V Blood carrier of the base progression and is widely considered the most challenging encounter in the game.

§Strategy

Solarus alternates between sword phases and crossbow phases, with holy damage zones covering much of the arena. Holy Resistance of at least 150 is strongly recommended, along with gear level 84 or higher. In his second phase he summons a Divine Angel — visited link shown here — which must be kited away from the boss.
